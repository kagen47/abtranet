ABTRANET GLOBAL SOLUTIONS

ABTRANET is a wholesale supplier of used, refurbished and new computer products to clients in West Africa. We have been involved in the wholesale computer industry since 2002. We specialize in surplus, used, lease-returned, refurbished and new computer products, including Personal computers, Laptops, Monitors, Peripherals, etc. in large and small quantities. We export a large volume of equipment to many countries on every continent. Most of our customers are long-time repeat buyers. We also welcome local resellers who would like to buy in any quantity from single pieces to pallet loads. Our emphasis is on good-quality, tested and working product. We stand behind everything we sell and we want our customers to be pleased with our product, since our success depends on your repeat business.
